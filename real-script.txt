What is ScholarStream?

ScholarStream is an AI-powered financial opportunity hub.
It uses modern tech to discover, match, and deliver personalized scholarships, hackathons, bounties, and grants to students in urgent financial need.
And equally helps them actually win those opportunities.
Let me show you how.


Intelligent Onboarding: Capturing Your Digital DNA

It starts with onboarding.
We don't just ask for a name or a GPA. We capture what I call your Academic DNA.
Your technical stack. Your passions. Your background and Your urgency.
Then we use Google's embedding-001 model to convert this entire profile into a 768-dimensional semantic vector.
we're literally mapping your identity into a space where AI can find the opportunities that truly fit you.



Real-Time Opportunity Discovery

The moment I click "continue" here, something interesting happens behind the scenes.
A fleet of stealth Playwright crawlers is dispatched across the web.
they hunt specifically for opportunities that match the vector we just created for your profile.

And as raw data pours in, it flows through our event-driven pipeline, and here is how it goes (the cortex pipeline):
- the stealth playwright crawlers help discover opportunities in form of raw html.
- we use Gemini to extract structured data from the raw HTML.
- And we've a processor that deduplicates, filters and classify these opportunities in real time into Hackathon, Bounty, Scholarship and Grant.
All happening within seconds.


Personalized Data in Motion

And before the data riches the dashboard.
Our backend takes the enriched, deduplicated results and runs them through the Matching Engine.
It scores every opportunity against your Digital DNA using our 70/30 Formula:
70% from cosine similarity between your vector and the opportunity's vector.
30% from hard-logic heuristic filters — location, GPA, skill keywords.
Then it pushes the highest matches directly to your dashboard via WebSocket.
No polling. No refreshing.
Just personalized opportunities appearing in real time.
You can filter by location, by platform, by type, and bookmark your favorites.
